Employee Performance Analysis
A company wants to analyze the performance and salary of its employees across multiple departments. The HR team has provided a CSV file containing the following columns:

Employee_ID: Unique identifier for each employee.

Name: Employee's name.

Department: The department they work in.

Experience: Years of experience.

Salary: Monthly salary.

Performance_Score: A score (out of 100) based on annual reviews.

Objectives:

Load and inspect the data.

Find employees with missing values and handle them.

Calculate average salary per department.

Find the top 3 highest-paid employees.

Identify employees with performance scores above 80.

Increase salary by 10% for employees with scores above 90.

Visualize salary distribution.
